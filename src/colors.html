    Color Swatches

    The signature color palette of Auto Data Direct, Inc. is Black and ADD Blue which creates a powerful differentiator for our brand. Using this palette appropriately and consistently creates an additional layer of distinction.

    Our signature colors may be used extensively both for large areas of color and as accent colors.

    Primary Colors

    $add-primary #0190cd

    $black #000000

    Supporting Colors

    The colors in our supporting palette were chosen to complement our primary palette of black and electric blue, providing additional range to the brand experience.

    All swatches adhere to WCAG 2.0 color accessibility requirements that outline the legibility of an object based on its foreground and background color contrast. See how these colors conform to the WCAG 2.0 standard.

    Screens or tints of the supporting colors may be used to achieve the desired effect but should be used cautiously, as screening certain colors may result in undesirable pastels.

    $color #A8E5FF

    $color #76D6FF

    $color #27B1EC

    $add-primary #0190cd

    $color #016895

    $color #01364d

    $color #00121a
